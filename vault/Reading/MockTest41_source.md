## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**From:** Melissa Grant <m.grant@harperlinetech.com>
**To:** All Staff <all-staff@harperlinetech.com>
**Date:** May 4, 2026
**Subject:** Join Our New Company Book Club!

Dear Colleagues,

I am pleased to announce that Harperline Tech is launching its very first Book Club, an initiative designed to foster creativity, encourage open discussion, and help our employees connect beyond their daily projects.

Our inaugural selection is *The Innovator's Path* by Daniel Okafor, a thought-provoking book on modern leadership and workplace culture. Members will meet **once a month** in the 4th-floor lounge, and our first gathering is scheduled for **Thursday, May 28, from 5:30 P.M. to 6:30 P.M.** Light refreshments will be provided.

To sign up, please complete the online form on the company intranet by **May 20**. Space is limited to 20 participants, so early registration is encouraged. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me directly at extension 412.

We look forward to reading and growing together!

Best regards,
Melissa Grant
HR Coordinator

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the e-mail?**
(A) To announce a change in company policy
(B) To invite employees to participate in a new activity
(C) To promote a book written by an employee
(D) To request feedback on workplace culture

**2. What should employees do if they want to join?**
(A) Send an e-mail to Ms. Grant
(B) Call extension 412 before May 20
(C) Fill out a form on the company intranet
(D) Attend the meeting on May 28

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** メール冒頭で "Harperline Tech is launching its very first Book Club" と新しい社内活動の立ち上げを告知し、"an initiative designed to foster creativity, encourage open discussion, and help our employees connect" と社員の参加を呼びかけている。よって新しい活動への参加を促すことが目的。(A) は方針変更の話ではないので不適。(C) は本を紹介しているが社員が書いたとは書かれていない。(D) フィードバック依頼ではない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 申し込み方法について "To sign up, please complete the online form on the company intranet by May 20." と明記されている。よって社内イントラネット上のフォームに記入する必要がある。(A) メール送信の指示はない。(B) extension 412 は質問用の連絡先であり申込先ではない。(D) ミーティングは申込後に参加するもので、参加自体が申込にはならない。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示

> - **initiative:** 新しい取り組み、構想

> - **inaugural:** 最初の、開始の
> - **thought-provoking:** 考えさせられる、示唆に富む
> - **gathering:** 集まり、会合
> - **refreshments:** 軽食、飲み物
> - **extension:** (電話の)内線番号
