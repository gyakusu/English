## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Help Wanted)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Join the Maple Street Coffee Family!**

Maple Street Coffee, a cozy specialty coffee shop located in downtown Brighton, is looking for a friendly **part-time barista** to join our small but passionate team.

**Position Details:**
- 20-25 hours per week, including at least one weekend shift
- Morning shifts start at 6:30 A.M.; evening shifts end by 8:00 P.M.
- Hourly wage: $16.00, plus a share of daily tips (typically $4-6 per hour)

**Responsibilities:**
- Preparing espresso-based drinks and pour-over coffee
- Greeting customers and taking orders
- Keeping the café clean and restocking supplies

**Requirements:**
Prior barista experience is preferred but not required. We are happy to provide full training to candidates who are reliable, eager to learn, and enjoy working with people.

**How to Apply:**
Please send your résumé and a short note about why you love coffee to jobs@maplestreetcoffee.com by **May 15, 2026**. Selected applicants will be invited for an in-person interview and a brief coffee-making trial.

We can't wait to meet you!

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is indicated about the position?**
(A) It requires at least two years of barista experience.
(B) It includes weekend work.
(C) It is a full-time management role.
(D) It offers a fixed monthly salary.

**2. According to the advertisement, what must applicants do to apply?**
(A) Visit the shop in person before May 15.
(B) Call the shop owner directly.
(C) Email a résumé and a personal note about coffee.
(D) Complete an online application form.

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 勤務時間の条件として "20-25 hours per week, including at least one weekend shift" と明記されており、週末のシフトが少なくとも1回含まれることがわかります。よって (B) It includes weekend work. が正解です。(A) は "Prior barista experience is preferred but not required"(経験は望ましいが必須ではない)と矛盾します。(C) は "part-time barista" とあるため不適切。(D) は "Hourly wage: $16.00" と時給制なので誤りです。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 応募方法について "Please send your résumé and a short note about why you love coffee to jobs@maplestreetcoffee.com" とあり、履歴書とコーヒーについての短いメモをEメールで送るよう指示されています。したがって (C) が正解。(A) の来店や (B) の電話、(D) のオンラインフォームについては本文に記載がありません。
