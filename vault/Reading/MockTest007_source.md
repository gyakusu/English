## 📝 Part 7: Double Passage (Job Posting + Cover Letter)
**Time Goal:** 5 mins

### [Passage A: Job Posting]

**Position: Marketing Coordinator**
**Company: Lumino Creative Agency**
**Location: Sydney, Australia (Hybrid)**

Lumino Creative Agency is seeking a motivated Marketing Coordinator to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will support our marketing department in developing campaigns across digital and print media.

**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate marketing projects and liaise with clients
- Manage social media accounts and analyze engagement metrics
- Assist in the preparation of proposals and presentations

**Requirements:**
- Bachelor's degree in Marketing or a related field
- Minimum 2 years of experience in a marketing role
- Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office
- Strong written and verbal communication skills

Applications accepted through May 10. Please submit your CV and a cover letter to careers@luminocreative.com.

---

### [Passage B: Cover Letter]

**From:** Priya Mehta
**To:** careers@luminocreative.com
**Date:** April 22, 2026
**Subject:** Application for Marketing Coordinator Position

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Marketing Coordinator position advertised by Lumino Creative Agency. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Communications and have worked in marketing roles for the past three years, most recently as a Junior Marketing Specialist at Horizon Media Group.

In my current role, I have managed multiple social media platforms and created detailed analytics reports to improve campaign performance. I am proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and have led several cross-functional projects.

I am particularly drawn to Lumino's reputation for bold, innovative design campaigns. I believe my background aligns well with your requirements, and I would welcome the opportunity to contribute to your team.

Please find my CV attached. I am available for an interview at your earliest convenience.

Kind regards,
Priya Mehta

---

### [Questions]

**1. Which job requirement does Ms. Mehta clearly meet based on her cover letter?**
(A) Having a degree specifically in Marketing
(B) Having at least two years of experience in a marketing role
(C) Currently residing in Sydney, Australia
(D) Having experience in print media production

**2. What does Ms. Mehta mention about her responsibilities in her current position?**
(A) She manages a team of junior graphic designers
(B) She creates analytics reports for social media campaigns
(C) She is responsible for hiring new marketing staff
(D) She previously worked at Lumino Creative Agency

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 求人票では "Minimum 2 years of experience" が条件。Mehta氏は "worked in marketing roles for the past three years" と述べており、条件を超えて満たしています。(A)の「Marketing専攻」については、彼女の専攻は "Communications" であるため厳密には一致しません（"related field" には該当しますが、「clearly meet」の観点では(B)がより明確）。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** カバーレターの第2段落に "created detailed analytics reports to improve campaign performance" とあります。(A)(C)(D)はいずれも本文に記載がありません。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Liaise:** 連絡を取る、折衝する
> - **Engagement metrics:** エンゲージメント指標（いいね・シェア数など）
> - **Proficiency:** 能力、習熟度
> - **Cross-functional:** 部門横断的な
> - **Drawn to:** 〜に魅力を感じる
> - **Align with:** 〜と一致する、合致する
> - **Analytics report:** 分析レポート
