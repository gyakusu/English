## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Notice)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**NOTICE TO ALL TENANTS — Harwood Tower**

**Temporary Closure of Underground Parking Lot**

Please be advised that Levels B1 and B2 of the Harwood Tower parking facility will be temporarily closed for resurfacing work from **Monday, May 11 through Friday, May 15, 2026**. Work will be carried out between **7:00 A.M. and 6:00 P.M. each day**. The repaving is part of our annual maintenance program and is intended to improve drainage and extend the life of the pavement.

During this period, tenants with monthly parking contracts may use the **Clarkson Street Public Garage**, located two blocks east of our building. Vehicles must display a **temporary validation ticket**, which can be collected from the security desk in the main lobby starting **May 4**. Please present your tenant ID card when requesting a ticket. Each company will be issued permits according to the number registered in its lease agreement.

Level B3 will remain open throughout the project for short-term visitor parking.

For questions, contact Building Services at extension 2210 or e-mail services@harwoodtower.com.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the notice?**
(A) To announce a change in monthly parking fees
(B) To inform tenants about a scheduled parking closure
(C) To advertise a newly opened public garage
(D) To request feedback on a maintenance program

**2. What are tenants required to do to use the alternative parking?**
(A) Sign a new lease agreement
(B) Pay an additional monthly fee
(C) Obtain a validation ticket from the security desk
(D) Register their vehicles online before May 4

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "Temporary Closure of Underground Parking Lot" と題し、"Levels B1 and B2 of the Harwood Tower parking facility will be temporarily closed for resurfacing work" と述べています。つまり、テナントに対して予定されている駐車場閉鎖を知らせることが目的です。(A) 料金変更の話はありません。(C) 代替として Clarkson Street Public Garage が紹介されているだけで宣伝ではありません。(D) 意見の募集もされていません。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文に "Vehicles must display a temporary validation ticket, which can be collected from the security desk in the main lobby starting May 4." とあり、代替駐車場を利用するには security desk で validation ticket を受け取る必要があります。よって (C) が正解。(A)(B) は本文に記載なし。(D) 5月4日は ticket の配布開始日であり、オンライン登録の期限ではありません。
