## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Letter)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

April 18, 2026

Mr. Daniel Brooks
Customer Relations Manager
Hillview Grand Hotel
42 Marine Avenue, Brighton

Dear Mr. Brooks,

I am writing to express my disappointment regarding my recent stay at the Hillview Grand Hotel from April 10 to April 12, 2026 (Reservation #HG-77842).

Although your website advertises "premium comfort and attentive service," my experience fell far short of these promises. Upon arrival, I waited over forty minutes at the front desk, as only one staff member was on duty. The room I was eventually assigned had not been properly cleaned: the bathroom towels were damp, and the air conditioner produced a loud rattling noise throughout the night. When I contacted the front desk to request a room change, I was told that no other rooms were available.

Given these circumstances, I would like to request a full refund of my room charges. I have enclosed a copy of the invoice for your reference. I hope you will address these issues promptly so that future guests may enjoy the standard of service your hotel claims to provide.

Sincerely,

Margaret Chen
Senior Consultant, Linden & Associates

---

### [Questions]

**1. Why did Ms. Chen write the letter?**
(A) To reserve a room for a future business trip
(B) To praise a helpful hotel employee
(C) To complain about the quality of her stay
(D) To inquire about the hotel's membership program

**2. What problem is NOT mentioned in the letter?**
(A) A long wait at the front desk
(B) Rude behavior by hotel staff
(C) An unclean room
(D) A noisy air conditioner

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "I am writing to express my disappointment regarding my recent stay" と述べ、サービスの質について不満を訴えている。後半でも "I would like to request a full refund" とあり、苦情が目的。よって (C) が正解。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 本文では (A)「40分以上の待ち時間」"I waited over forty minutes at the front desk"、(C)「掃除されていない部屋」"The room... had not been properly cleaned"、(D)「うるさいエアコン」"the air conditioner produced a loud rattling noise" がすべて言及されている。しかしスタッフの無礼な態度については述べられていないため、(B) が正解。
