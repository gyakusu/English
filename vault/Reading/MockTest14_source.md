## 📝 Part 7: Double Passage (Conference Brochure + E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 5 mins

### [Passage 1: Conference Brochure]

**NorthRail Logistics Summit 2026**
**Date:** June 18–20 | **Venue:** The Crestfield Convention Centre, Manchester

Join over 1,200 logistics professionals for three days of keynotes, workshops, and networking focused on sustainable freight innovation.

**Registration Fees (per delegate):**
| Package | Early Bird (until May 1) | Standard (after May 1) |
|---------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| Full Pass (3 days) | £690 | £820 |
| Two-Day Pass | £520 | £640 |
| One-Day Pass | £280 | £340 |

**Group discount:** Organizations registering 5 or more delegates under a single booking receive an additional 15% off the applicable rate.

All passes include access to the exhibition hall, coffee breaks, and the Welcome Reception on the evening of June 17. The Gala Dinner on June 19 is offered as an optional add-on for £75.

Register at www.northrailsummit.com.

---

### [Passage 2: E-mail]

**From:** Javier Ortiz, Logistics Director, Cedro Freight
**To:** registration@northrailsummit.com
**Date:** April 30
**Subject:** Group registration — Cedro Freight (7 delegates)

Hello,

I would like to register seven delegates from Cedro Freight for the summit. All seven will attend the full three days. Additionally, four of them would like to attend the Gala Dinner.

Could you please confirm the total cost before I proceed with payment? I am submitting this e-mail today to ensure we qualify for the early pricing.

Please also let me know whether payment must be made in a single transaction, or whether we may split it across two corporate cards.

Kind regards,
Javier Ortiz

---

### [Questions]

**1. At what rate will Cedro Freight's delegates most likely be charged for the conference?**
(A) £820 per delegate, with no discount applied
(B) £690 per delegate, with no group discount
(C) £690 per delegate, minus an additional 15% discount
(D) £340 per delegate, under the one-day rate

**2. What does Mr. Ortiz specifically ask about in his e-mail?**
(A) Whether the venue can accommodate dietary restrictions
(B) Whether payment may be divided between two cards
(C) Whether a refund is available if a delegate cancels
(D) Whether the Welcome Reception has a separate fee

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Passage 1 では5月1日までの早割で Full Pass が £690、また5人以上のグループ登録には追加15%割引と記載。Passage 2 の日付は4月30日で7名のフル3日登録 → 両方の条件を満たすため、"£690 minus an additional 15%" が正解。(B)はグループ割引を無視しているので誤り。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Passage 2 の最後に "whether payment must be made in a single transaction, or whether we may split it across two corporate cards" とあります。"split it across two corporate cards" ＝ 2枚のカードに分割する、"divided between two cards" と一致。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Delegate:** 代表者、参加者
> - **Keynote:** 基調講演
> - **Freight:** 貨物、運送
> - **Early Bird (rate):** 早割料金
> - **Applicable:** 適用される
> - **Add-on:** 追加オプション
> - **Qualify for:** 〜の資格を得る
> - **Split across:** 〜に分割する

### 🧠 学習のポイント
> [!tip]- 複数条件の掛け合わせ問題
> 1. **日付の条件**: 4月30日送信 → 5月1日締切の早割に間に合う。
> 2. **人数の条件**: 7名 → 5名以上のグループ割引適用。
> 3. **両方の条件を同時に満たす**選択肢を選ぶ必要があります。片方だけに目を向けると(A)や(B)を選んでしまうので、ダブルパッセージでは「条件の重なり」を常に意識してください。
