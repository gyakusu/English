## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]
**From:** Daniel Roberts, Operations Manager
**To:** All Marketing Department Staff
**Date:** June 3, 2026
**Subject:** Upcoming Office Relocation

Dear Team,

I am writing to inform you that our department will be relocating to the newly constructed Harbor View Tower on Monday, July 13. The new office, located on the 15th floor of the building at 240 Bayside Avenue, offers significantly more workspace and modern meeting facilities.

To ensure a smooth transition, please pack all personal belongings and non-essential documents into the labeled boxes that will be distributed on July 6. Each box must be clearly marked with your name and desk number. The moving company, Swift Relocations Inc., will transport all items on the evening of July 10.

Please note that our office phones and internet connections will be temporarily unavailable from July 10 through July 12. During this period, staff are encouraged to work remotely.

If you have any questions regarding the relocation, feel free to contact my assistant, Ms. Laura Chen, at extension 402.

Best regards,
Daniel Roberts

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the e-mail?**
(A) To introduce a new operations manager
(B) To announce a change in office location
(C) To request feedback on a moving company
(D) To describe a new remote work policy

**2. What are employees asked to do before July 10?**
(A) Contact Ms. Laura Chen directly
(B) Visit the new building in advance
(C) Submit a list of personal belongings
(D) Pack their items into labeled boxes

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** メール冒頭で "our department will be relocating to the newly constructed Harbor View Tower on Monday, July 13" と述べており、オフィスの移転を知らせることが主な目的。よって (B) が正解。(A) Daniel Robertsは差出人であり新任紹介ではない。(C) 引越し業者への feedback は求められていない。(D) リモートワークは移転期間中の一時的措置にすぎない。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** 本文に "please pack all personal belongings and non-essential documents into the labeled boxes that will be distributed on July 6" とあり、従業員は荷物をラベル付きの箱に詰めるよう指示されている。引越しは July 10 に行われるため、それ以前の作業となる。よって (D) が正解。(A) Ms. Chenへの連絡は質問がある場合のみ。(B)(C) は本文に記述なし。
