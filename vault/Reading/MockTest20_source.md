## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**From:** Aarav Singh, Events Coordinator, Riverside Community Library
**To:** Volunteer List
**Date:** April 13, 2026
**Subject:** Volunteers needed — Spring Book Fair (May 23)

Dear Volunteers,

We are now finalizing preparations for our annual Spring Book Fair, which will take place on Saturday, May 23, from 9:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M. in the library's east wing.

We are looking for volunteers to help in three areas:

- **Set-up team (7:30 A.M. – 9:00 A.M.):** arranging tables and organizing donated books by category.
- **Floor assistants (three shifts, 2 hours each):** helping visitors locate books and assisting at the checkout counter.
- **Clean-up team (5:00 P.M. – 6:30 P.M.):** packing unsold books for donation and resetting the hall.

Each volunteer may sign up for more than one role, but please be mindful of pacing yourself. Lunch vouchers for the library cafe will be provided to anyone volunteering for more than four hours in total.

To sign up, please reply to this e-mail by April 30 with your preferred role(s) and any time constraints. New volunteers are welcome — no experience required.

Thank you for your continued support of our community programs.

Warm regards,
Aarav Singh

---

### [Questions]

**1. What task will set-up volunteers be expected to perform?**
(A) Greet authors arriving for the event
(B) Sort donated books into categories
(C) Operate the cash register at checkout
(D) Pack unsold books for donation

**2. What will a volunteer receive if they contribute over four hours of their time?**
(A) A small cash honorarium
(B) A free book of their choice
(C) Priority registration for next year's event
(D) A voucher to use at the library cafe

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** "Set-up team" の説明に "arranging tables and organizing donated books by category" とあります。"organizing by category" ＝ "sort into categories" の言い換え。(D) はClean-up teamの業務。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** "Lunch vouchers for the library cafe will be provided to anyone volunteering for more than four hours in total" とあります。"lunch vouchers for the library cafe" ＝ "voucher to use at the library cafe" と一致。
