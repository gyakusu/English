## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Online Review)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**★★★★☆ — "A pleasant stay, with one caveat"**
**Reviewer:** Daniel Okafor | **Hotel:** The Seabreeze Inn, Brighton
**Posted:** April 10, 2026

I booked a two-night stay at The Seabreeze Inn while attending a conference in Brighton. Overall, the hotel exceeded my expectations. The rooms were spacious, the bedding was comfortable, and the ocean view from the balcony was genuinely breathtaking. Staff at the front desk were attentive and handled my late check-in without any issues.

Breakfast was included in the rate and offered a reasonable variety, though I wish there had been more hot options. My one real complaint concerns the Wi-Fi: the connection in my room was so unstable that I had to move to the lobby to join a video meeting. For a hotel that markets itself to business travelers, this is something management should address urgently.

I would still recommend The Seabreeze Inn for a leisure trip, but business travelers may want to confirm connectivity before booking.

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第1段落に "Staff at the front desk were attentive and handled my late check-in without any issues" とあります。"attentive" ＝ 気配りのある、"responsiveness" と言い換えられています。(A)は "wish there had been more hot options" とむしろ不満が述べられています。
>
> **2. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "the Wi-Fi: the connection in my room was so unstable" "this is something management should address urgently" とあります。"address urgently" ＝ 早急に対処すべき問題として挙げているのはWi-Fi（インターネット接続）です。
