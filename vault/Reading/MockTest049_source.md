## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**From:** Melissa Carrington <m.carrington@brightwave-retail.com>
**To:** David Okonkwo <d.okonkwo@okonkwo-consulting.com>
**Date:** April 17, 2026
**Subject:** Rescheduling our strategy session

Dear Mr. Okonkwo,

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to apologize for the short notice, but I must request that we reschedule our strategy meeting originally set for Tuesday, April 21, at 10:00 A.M. at our downtown office. Our regional director has unexpectedly called a mandatory leadership briefing at that exact time, and I am unable to step away from it.

Would either of the following alternatives work for you? I could meet on Thursday, April 23, at 2:00 P.M., or on Friday, April 24, anytime between 9:00 A.M. and noon. I am happy to host you at our office again, or to come to yours if that is more convenient.

Please confirm which option suits your schedule. When we meet, kindly bring the updated market analysis and the revised pricing proposal we discussed last week, as the board will need both documents by month-end.

Thank you for your flexibility and understanding.

Best regards,
Melissa Carrington
Director of Operations, Brightwave Retail

---

### [Questions]

**1. Why did Ms. Carrington write the e-mail?**
(A) To cancel a consulting contract
(B) To request additional documents
(C) To postpone a scheduled meeting
(D) To introduce a new director

**2. What is Mr. Okonkwo asked to do?**
(A) Prepare a new pricing proposal from scratch
(B) Confirm a new meeting time
(C) Attend a leadership briefing
(D) Send the market analysis by e-mail

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "I must request that we reschedule our strategy meeting originally set for Tuesday, April 21" と述べており、元々予定されていた会議を延期したい旨を伝えるためのメールです。reschedule = postpone（延期する）の言い換え。(A) 契約解除の話はしていません。(B) 資料持参の依頼はありますが、メールの主目的ではありません。(D) 「regional director」は言及されていますが紹介が目的ではありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 後半に "Please confirm which option suits your schedule." とあり、提示された2つの候補日時（木曜14時 または金曜9時〜正午）のどちらが都合が良いか確認してほしいと依頼しています。したがって「新しい会議時間を確定する」(B) が正解。(A) 価格提案は "the revised pricing proposal we discussed last week" と既に議論済みのものを持参するよう求めており、ゼロから作るわけではありません。(C) リーダーシップ会議に出席するのは Carrington 氏本人です。(D) 資料は郵送ではなく「持参」を求めています（"kindly bring"）。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **reschedule:** (予定を)変更する、延期する
> - **on short notice:** 急な連絡で、直前の通知で
> - **mandatory:** 必須の、義務的な
> - **step away from ~:** 〜から離れる、中座する
> - **alternative:** 代替案、選択肢
> - **suit one's schedule:** 〜の都合に合う
> - **revised:** 修正された、改訂された
> - **flexibility:** 柔軟性、融通
