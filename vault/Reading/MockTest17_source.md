## 📝 Part 7: Double Passage (Complaint Letter + Reply E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 5 mins

### [Passage 1: Complaint Letter]

**Ms. Angela Ferraro**
28 Linden Avenue, Birmingham B15 2TH
April 9, 2026

**Customer Relations**
Crestview Home Appliances Ltd.
London Road Industrial Estate, Reading RG2 0TE

Dear Customer Relations,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the service I received after purchasing a Crestview EcoCool refrigerator (Model RC-445) from your authorized dealer in Birmingham on February 14, 2026.

Within six weeks of delivery, the unit began producing a persistent humming noise. I contacted your service line on March 27 and was assured that a technician would visit within five working days. To date, more than ten working days have passed, and no technician has arrived, nor have I received any follow-up communication.

I would appreciate either an immediate service visit or, failing that, a full refund. Please respond within seven days of receipt.

Sincerely,
Angela Ferraro

---

### [Passage 2: Reply E-mail]

**From:** Derek Mwangi, Customer Relations Manager, Crestview Home Appliances
**To:** Angela Ferraro
**Date:** April 14, 2026
**Subject:** Response to your complaint dated April 9

Dear Ms. Ferraro,

Thank you for taking the time to write to us, and please accept our sincere apologies for the delay in service. Upon reviewing our records, I found that your service request was misfiled due to a system error that affected a number of cases in late March.

I have personally escalated your case. A senior technician will visit your home on April 17 between 10:00 A.M. and 12:00 P.M. to inspect and repair the unit. As a gesture of goodwill, we would also like to offer you a complimentary 24-month extended warranty on your refrigerator, in addition to the standard warranty that remains in effect.

If the April 17 visit is not convenient, please reply to this e-mail and we will reschedule at your preference.

Once again, I apologize for the inconvenience.

Kind regards,
Derek Mwangi

---

### [Questions]

**1. Why did Ms. Ferraro's service request not receive a timely response?**
(A) The technician was reassigned to a higher-priority case
(B) Her request was misplaced due to an internal system problem
(C) The part needed for the repair was out of stock
(D) She had not paid the service visit fee in advance

**2. What does Mr. Mwangi offer Ms. Ferraro in addition to the repair visit?**
(A) A full refund for the refrigerator
(B) A discount on her next purchase
(C) A free cleaning and maintenance session
(D) A 24-month extension of her warranty

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Passage 2 に "your service request was misfiled due to a system error" とあります。"misfiled" ＝ 間違って処理された ＝ "misplaced"、"system error" ＝ 内部システムの問題。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** Passage 2 に "we would also like to offer you a complimentary 24-month extended warranty" とあります。"complimentary" ＝ 無料の、"extended warranty" ＝ 保証延長。(A) の返金は Passage 1 で Ms. Ferraro が求めた選択肢ですが、会社は修理 + 保証延長で対応しています。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Dissatisfaction:** 不満
> - **Persistent:** 持続的な、止まない
> - **Assure:** 保証する、請け合う
> - **To date:** 現在までに
> - **Escalate:** 上位部門に上げる、対応を強化する
> - **As a gesture of goodwill:** 誠意の印として
> - **Misfile:** 間違って処理する／ファイルする
> - **In effect:** 有効である

### 🧠 学習のポイント
> [!tip]- 苦情 → 対応 の典型フロー
> 1. **苦情文（Passage 1）では何を要求しているか** を把握（ここでは「修理訪問 or 全額返金」）。
> 2. **返信（Passage 2）がどの要求を受け入れ、何を追加で提供するか** を把握。
> 3. **追加のオファー（goodwill gesture）** はTOEIC頻出で、設問2のような形で狙われます。
