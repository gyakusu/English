### [Questions]

**1. What is required for Morningside Cafe to qualify for the early-payment discount?**
(A) Paying the invoice by the due date
(B) Placing a repeat order within 30 days
(C) Paying within 10 days and citing the invoice number
(D) Contacting Sophie Grant in advance

**2. Why was a rush production fee included in the invoice?**
(A) The order exceeded the standard quantity limit
(B) The customer asked for a very short turnaround
(C) The print shop was operating outside of business hours
(D) The items required special laminating equipment

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Note に "Payments received within 10 days of the issue date are eligible for a 3% early-payment discount. To claim the discount, please reference your invoice number when making payment" とあります。10日以内の支払い と 請求書番号の明記、両方が必要。(A)は due date（期日）では早割対象外。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Note に "the rush production fee was added because the order was submitted with a requested turnaround of under 48 hours" とあります。"under 48 hours" ＝ 非常に短納期 ＝ "very short turnaround" の言い換え。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Invoice:** 請求書
> - **Due date:** 支払期日
> - **Net-30:** 30日後払いの条件
> - **Be eligible for:** 〜の資格がある
> - **Reference (a number):** （番号を）明記する
> - **Turnaround:** 納期、回転時間
> - **Non-refundable:** 払い戻し不可の
> - **Rush fee:** 特急料金
