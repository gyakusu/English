## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Text Message Chain)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Rebecca Hayes** (10:38 A.M.)
Daniel, I just got off the phone with the client. They noticed we didn't submit the Phase 2 report yesterday. They sounded pretty disappointed.

**Daniel Carter** (10:40 A.M.)
I was afraid of that. The data team sent me the final numbers only last night. I take full responsibility.

**Rebecca Hayes** (10:42 A.M.)
Let's not dwell on that now. Can you send a draft by end of day? I'll stay late to review it.

**Daniel Carter** (10:44 A.M.)
Absolutely. I'll skip the marketing meeting at 2 so I can focus. Should I CC Mr. Tanaka on the revised version?

**Rebecca Hayes** (10:46 A.M.)
Please do. And attach a short note apologizing for the delay — keep it brief but sincere.

**Daniel Carter** (10:47 A.M.)
Got it. You'll have the draft in your inbox before 6.

---

### [Questions]

**1. At 10:42 A.M., what does Ms. Hayes most likely mean when she writes, "Let's not dwell on that now"?**
(A) She wants Mr. Carter to forget about the client entirely.
(B) She thinks the client's complaint was unfair.
(C) She prefers to focus on fixing the problem rather than assigning blame.
(D) She plans to discuss the issue in a later meeting.

**2. What will Mr. Carter most likely do next?**
(A) Call Mr. Tanaka to apologize in person.
(B) Prepare a draft of the Phase 2 report.
(C) Attend the marketing meeting at 2 P.M.
(D) Request additional data from the data team.

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Daniel が "I take full responsibility"(全責任は自分にある)と述べた直後に、Rebecca は "Let's not dwell on that now. Can you send a draft by end of day?" と返している。"dwell on 〜" は「〜をくよくよ考える／こだわる」の意味で、責任追及にこだわらず、今日中にドラフトを送るという「問題の修正」へ話題を切り替えている。したがって、責任を問うよりも問題解決に集中したい、という (C) が正解。(A) はクライアントを忘れろとまでは言っておらず不適切。(B) クライアントの不満が不当とは一切述べていない。(D) 後日の会議で話し合う言及はない。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Rebecca が "Can you send a draft by end of day?" と依頼し、Daniel は "Absolutely." と承諾、さらに "I'll skip the marketing meeting at 2 so I can focus." と述べている。最後に "You'll have the draft in your inbox before 6." と確約している。よって次にやることは Phase 2 レポートのドラフト作成、(B) が正解。(A) Mr. Tanaka は CC するだけで電話はしない。(C) マーケティング会議はスキップする。(D) データは昨夜すでに受領済み。

### 🔑 重要語彙
