## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Press Release)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**VeloGreen Launches the World's First Solar-Assisted City Bicycle**
**Amsterdam — April 11, 2026**

VeloGreen Mobility, the Amsterdam-based urban transport company, today unveiled the VG-Sol, a new category of city bicycle that supplements rider effort with energy drawn from flexible solar panels integrated into the frame. The VG-Sol is designed for daily commuters who cycle between four and fifteen kilometres each way.

Unlike conventional electric bicycles, the VG-Sol does not require regular plug-in charging under typical conditions. According to independent testing, a single day of moderate sunshine provides sufficient energy for approximately 25 kilometres of power-assisted riding. For cyclists in regions with limited sunlight, the bicycle also includes a conventional charging port as a backup.

"Our goal is to make zero-emission commuting effortless," said Luka Peeters, VeloGreen's Chief Product Officer. "The VG-Sol is the first product of its kind, and we expect strong interest from municipalities piloting sustainable transport programs."

The VG-Sol will be available in select European markets starting July 1, 2026, with a recommended retail price of €2,890. Pre-orders open April 20 at www.velogreen.eu.

### [Questions]

**1. How does the VG-Sol primarily differ from a traditional electric bicycle?**
(A) It is significantly lighter in weight
(B) It can be ridden without any rider effort
(C) It is sold at a lower retail price
(D) It typically does not need to be plugged in to charge

**2. Who does Mr. Peeters expect to show particular interest in the product?**
(A) Professional cycling teams
(B) City governments promoting green transport
(C) Rural delivery companies
(D) University research laboratories

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "the VG-Sol does not require regular plug-in charging under typical conditions" とあります。"does not require plug-in charging" ＝ "does not need to be plugged in"。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 第3段落に "we expect strong interest from municipalities piloting sustainable transport programs" とあります。"municipalities" ＝ 地方自治体 ＝ "city governments"、"sustainable transport programs" ＝ "green transport"。
