## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Memo)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** All Harborline Logistics Staff
**From:** Daniel Pereira, IT Department
**Date:** May 14, 2026
**Subject:** Scheduled Overnight System Maintenance

Please be advised that the IT Department will carry out essential maintenance on our internal network and the Orion shipment-tracking platform on **Friday, May 22, from 10:00 P.M. to 4:30 A.M. the following morning**. During this window, email, the company intranet, and all shared drives will be unavailable.

Before leaving the office on Friday, every employee is required to complete three steps. First, save and close any documents stored on the shared drives. Second, log out of the Orion system; sessions left open may be terminated automatically and could result in data loss. Third, back up critical local files to your assigned cloud folder.

Services are expected to be fully restored by **5:00 A.M. on Saturday**. Should you encounter any issues when logging in on Monday morning, please contact the helpdesk at extension 4421 or submit a ticket through the support portal. We appreciate your cooperation.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is one purpose of the memo?**
(A) To announce the launch of a new tracking platform
(B) To invite staff to an IT training session
(C) To inform employees about a temporary service outage
(D) To request feedback on recent software updates

**2. What are employees instructed to do before leaving on Friday?**
(A) Shut down their office computers completely
(B) Back up important files to a cloud folder
(C) Submit a helpdesk ticket in advance
(D) Forward pending emails to their supervisor

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** メモ冒頭で "the IT Department will carry out essential maintenance on our internal network and the Orion shipment-tracking platform" と述べ、さらに "During this window, email, the company intranet, and all shared drives will be unavailable." と一時的にサービスが使えなくなることを伝えている。つまり (C)「従業員に一時的なサービス停止を知らせる」が目的。(A)は新プラットフォームの立ち上げではなく既存システムのメンテナンス、(B)研修の案内はなく、(D)フィードバックの要請も書かれていない。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 金曜退社前に行う3つのステップのうち、3番目に "back up critical local files to your assigned cloud folder" とあり、重要なローカルファイルをクラウドフォルダにバックアップするよう指示されている。よって (B) が正解。(A)「PCを完全にシャットダウン」とは書かれておらず、(C)「ヘルプデスクにチケットを提出」は月曜のログイン時に問題が起きた場合の対応、(D)メール転送の指示もない。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **carry out:** (計画・作業を)実行する
> - **essential maintenance:** 必要不可欠なメンテナンス
> - **unavailable:** 利用できない
> - **log out of ~:** ~からログアウトする
> - **terminate:** 終了させる、打ち切る
> - **back up:** バックアップを取る
> - **restore:** 復旧する、元に戻す
> - **submit a ticket:** (サポート用の)チケットを提出する
