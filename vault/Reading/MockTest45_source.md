## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Article)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Riverside Farmers' Market Draws Record Crowd**
*By Megan Holloway — Greendale Daily News, June 14, 2026*

The monthly Riverside Farmers' Market, held on the second Saturday of every month at Willow Park, welcomed more than 2,500 visitors last weekend — the highest turnout since the event launched in 2022. Organizers credit the record attendance to a wave of new vendors who joined the market this spring.

Among the fifteen newcomers are Brighton Bee Apiary, offering raw honey and beeswax candles, and Sato Noodle House, a family-run stall serving handmade udon. "We wanted to bring something our neighbors could not easily find at a supermarket," said Kenji Sato, who moved to Greendale last year.

Market coordinator Laura Chen noted that the variety has transformed the atmosphere. "Shoppers are staying longer and discovering producers they had never heard of," she said.

Residents interested in becoming vendors can submit an application through the market's website by the end of each month. The next market will be held on July 11, rain or shine.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the article?**
(A) To announce the opening of a new supermarket in Greendale
(B) To criticize the management of a local event
(C) To report on the growing success of a monthly market
(D) To invite readers to a charity fundraiser

**2. According to the article, how can residents become vendors?**
(A) By visiting Willow Park on a Saturday
(B) By applying online before the end of the month
(C) By contacting Kenji Sato directly
(D) By attending a meeting on July 11

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 記事は Riverside Farmers' Market が "the highest turnout since the event launched in 2022" を記録し、"a wave of new vendors" によって賑わいを増していることを報じている。見出し "Draws Record Crowd" も併せ、月例市場の好調ぶりを伝えるのが主目的。よって (C) が正解。(A) はスーパーの話ではないので不可、(B) 批判的な内容ではない、(D) 募金イベントの記載はない。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 最終段落に "Residents interested in becoming vendors can submit an application through the market's website by the end of each month." とあり、オンライン申請+月末までという条件が明記されている。これを言い換えた (B) が正解。(A)(C)(D) は本文に該当する記述がない。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **turnout:** 人出、参加者数
> - **vendor:** 出店者、販売業者
> - **credit A to B:** AをBのおかげだとする
> - **newcomer:** 新規参入者、新顔
> - **coordinator:** まとめ役、調整担当者
> - **submit an application:** 申請書を提出する
> - **rain or shine:** 雨でも晴れでも(開催する)
> - **atmosphere:** 雰囲気
