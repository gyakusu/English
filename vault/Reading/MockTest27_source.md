## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Announcement)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE — VOLUNTARY PRODUCT RECALL**

BrightHome Appliances Ltd. is voluntarily recalling its **ToastPro 450 Electric Toaster**, sold nationwide between September 2025 and February 2026. This recall affects units with serial numbers ranging from **TP450-0925001 to TP450-0226900**. The serial number can be found on the label attached to the bottom of the unit.

We have received several reports indicating that the heating element in the affected units may overheat during extended use, posing a potential risk of fire or burn injury. Although no serious injuries have been reported to date, we are taking this precautionary measure to ensure the safety of our customers.

Consumers who own an affected unit should **stop using the product immediately**, unplug it, and contact our Customer Care Center to arrange for a full refund or a free replacement with the updated ToastPro 460 model. Proof of purchase is not required.

Call 1-800-555-0142 (weekdays, 9 a.m.–6 p.m.) or visit www.brighthome.com/recall for instructions on how to return the product at no cost. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the announcement?**
(A) To promote a newly released toaster model
(B) To inform customers about a safety-related recall
(C) To announce a seasonal discount on appliances
(D) To request feedback on a recent product survey

**2. What are affected customers asked to do?**
(A) Send the product back along with the original receipt
(B) Wait for a technician to visit their home
(C) Stop using the toaster and contact the company
(D) Exchange the product at any retail store

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** タイトルに "IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE — VOLUNTARY PRODUCT RECALL" とあり、本文でも "BrightHome Appliances Ltd. is voluntarily recalling its ToastPro 450 Electric Toaster" と明言している。さらに "the heating element ... may overheat ... posing a potential risk of fire or burn injury" と安全上の問題に触れているため、主な目的は「安全に関わるリコールを顧客に知らせること」である(B)が正解。(A)は新製品の販促ではないので誤り、(C)は割引について述べていない、(D)はアンケートに関する記述はない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文中段に "Consumers who own an affected unit should stop using the product immediately, unplug it, and contact our Customer Care Center" と書かれている。つまり「使用を中止して会社に連絡する」ことが求められているので(C)が正解。(A)は "Proof of purchase is not required."(購入証明は不要)とあるため誤り。(B)技術者が訪問するとは書かれていない。(D)小売店での交換ではなく、Customer Care Center 経由での返品・交換である。
