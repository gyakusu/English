## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Memo)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** All Department Heads
**From:** Naomi Carter, Director of Human Resources
**Date:** May 14, 2026
**Subject:** Annual Team-Building Retreat

I am pleased to announce that our annual team-building retreat will take place on Friday, June 19, at the Lakeside Conference Center in Harwood. This year's program is designed to strengthen cross-departmental collaboration ahead of the third-quarter product launch.

The day will begin at 9:00 A.M. with a keynote session led by external facilitator Dr. Marcus Lim, followed by group problem-solving workshops. A buffet lunch and afternoon refreshments will be provided on-site at no cost to participants. The retreat will conclude with a networking dinner at 6:30 P.M.

Please forward the attached registration form to every member of your team and collect their responses by **Friday, May 29**. Late submissions cannot be accommodated due to catering requirements.

A chartered bus will depart from the main office lobby at 7:45 A.M. Staff who prefer to drive should contact Reception to reserve a parking permit in advance.

---

### [Questions]

**1. Why was the memo written?**
(A) To request feedback on a recent workshop
(B) To inform managers about an upcoming company event
(C) To introduce a newly hired facilitator
(D) To announce a change in office parking rules

**2. What are department heads asked to do by May 29?**
(A) Submit a budget proposal for the retreat
(B) Reserve seats on the chartered bus
(C) Gather their team members' responses
(D) Contact Dr. Lim directly

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "I am pleased to announce that our annual team-building retreat will take place on Friday, June 19" と述べ、社内の年次研修イベントを部長たちに通知している。よって「今後の社内イベントについて管理職に知らせる」とする (B) が正解。(A) は本文に言及なし。(C) は Dr. Lim への言及はあるが紹介が主目的ではない。(D) の駐車規則変更も本文にない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第3段落に "Please forward the attached registration form to every member of your team and collect their responses by Friday, May 29" とあり、部下の回答を集めるよう依頼している。したがって (C) "Gather their team members' responses" が正解。(B) のバス予約や (D) の Dr. Lim への連絡は指示されていない。(A) の予算提出も本文にない。
