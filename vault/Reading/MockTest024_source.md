## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Memo)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** All Headquarters Staff
**From:** Diane Whitaker, Director of Human Resources
**Date:** May 4, 2026
**Subject:** Updated Office Dress Code

Following feedback from last month's employee survey, management has decided to revise our current dress code. Effective Monday, June 1, all employees working at the headquarters building may adopt business-casual attire from Monday through Thursday. Acceptable items include collared shirts, blouses, chinos, knee-length skirts, and closed-toe shoes. Denim jeans, athletic wear, and sandals remain prohibited at all times.

Please note that formal business attire is still required on Fridays, as this is when most client visits take place. Employees scheduled to meet external clients or attend off-site conferences on any other day should also dress formally for those occasions.

Staff in the warehouse and maintenance divisions are not affected by this change and should continue to follow the safety-apparel guidelines outlined in their department manuals.

If you have questions regarding specific items, please contact the HR office at extension 204 before the policy takes effect.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the memo?**
(A) To remind staff about an upcoming client visit
(B) To inform employees of a change in workplace attire rules
(C) To request feedback on a recent employee survey
(D) To announce a new safety policy for warehouse workers

**2. According to the memo, when are employees still required to wear formal business attire?**
(A) Every Monday morning
(B) Only during annual company events
(C) On Fridays and when meeting clients
(D) Whenever they enter the headquarters building

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** メモの主題は冒頭の "management has decided to revise our current dress code" と "Effective Monday, June 1, all employees ... may adopt business-casual attire" から、服装規定(dress code)の変更を従業員に知らせることだと分かります。よって (B) が正解。(A) クライアント訪問は例外条件として触れられているだけ。(C) アンケートは変更のきっかけにすぎず、今回のメモで意見を募集しているわけではない。(D) 倉庫部門は「影響を受けない」と書かれています。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文中に "formal business attire is still required on Fridays" と明記され、さらに "Employees scheduled to meet external clients ... should also dress formally" とあるため、(C) が正解。(A)(B)(D) は本文に記述がありません。特に (D) は月〜木はビジネスカジュアルが許可されるので誤り。
