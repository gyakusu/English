## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Announcement)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**BrightWave Electronics Launches New Rewards Program**

BrightWave Electronics is pleased to announce the launch of our brand-new customer loyalty program, BrightWave Rewards, starting on June 1, 2026. Designed to thank our loyal shoppers, the program offers three membership tiers based on annual spending.

- **Silver** (spend $200 or more per year): Earn 1 point for every dollar spent, plus exclusive access to weekly member-only coupons.
- **Gold** (spend $800 or more per year): All Silver benefits, a 5% discount on selected home appliances, and free standard shipping on online orders.
- **Platinum** (spend $2,000 or more per year): All Gold benefits, a dedicated customer service line, early access to seasonal sales, and one complimentary in-home installation per year.

Enrollment is free and takes less than two minutes. Simply visit any BrightWave store or register online at www.brightwave-rewards.com. As a special launch promotion, customers who enroll before July 31, 2026 will automatically receive 500 bonus points, regardless of their tier.

For full terms and conditions, please refer to our website.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the announcement?**
(A) To advertise a summer sale on home appliances
(B) To introduce a new customer loyalty program
(C) To notify members of a change in store hours
(D) To recruit staff for a new retail location

**2. What benefit is available ONLY to Platinum members?**
(A) Member-only weekly coupons
(B) Free standard shipping on online orders
(C) Complimentary in-home installation
(D) One point for every dollar spent

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "BrightWave Electronics is pleased to announce the launch of our brand-new customer loyalty program, BrightWave Rewards" と述べられており、新しい顧客ロイヤルティプログラムの開始を告知することが目的です。よって (B) が正解。(A) のセールや (C) の営業時間変更、(D) の求人についての記述はありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Platinum 会員の特典として "one complimentary in-home installation per year"（年1回無料の自宅設置サービス）が挙げられており、これは他のティアには含まれていない Platinum 専用特典です。(A) は Silver 以上全員、(B) は Gold 以上、(D) は Silver から利用できるため、Platinum 限定ではありません。
