## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Job Posting)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Marketing Coordinator — Brightwave Solutions, Inc.**
**Location:** Chicago, IL (Hybrid — 3 days in office)

Brightwave Solutions, a growing provider of cloud-based analytics tools, is seeking a motivated Marketing Coordinator to join our team. The successful candidate will support campaign planning and help strengthen our brand presence across digital channels.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate the execution of email, social media, and webinar campaigns
- Track performance metrics and prepare weekly reports for senior staff
- Collaborate with the design team to produce promotional materials
- Maintain the company's content calendar and update the corporate website

**Requirements:**
- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field
- A minimum of three years of relevant professional experience
- Strong written communication skills and attention to detail
- Proficiency with Google Analytics and major social media platforms

**Compensation:** $58,000–$68,000 annually, plus comprehensive health insurance, a 401(k) match, and 20 paid vacation days.

To apply, please submit your résumé and a cover letter to careers@brightwavesol.com no later than **May 29, 2026**. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is indicated about the position?**
(A) It is fully remote.
(B) It requires some on-site work each week.
(C) It is a temporary contract role.
(D) It involves frequent international travel.

**2. What is NOT listed as a responsibility of the Marketing Coordinator?**
(A) Producing weekly performance reports
(B) Working with designers on promotional content
(C) Managing the department's annual budget
(D) Updating the company's website

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Location 欄に "Hybrid — 3 days in office" とあり、週に数日オフィス勤務が必要であることが分かります。よって "requires some on-site work each week" とする (B) が正解。"fully remote"（完全リモート）ではないので (A) は不適切。期間限定や国際出張に関する記述はないため (C)(D) も誤り。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Key Responsibilities には "prepare weekly reports"（→A）、"Collaborate with the design team to produce promotional materials"（→B）、"update the corporate website"（→D）が挙げられています。しかし "Managing the department's annual budget"（部門予算の管理）については本文に一切言及がないため、(C) が正解。NOT 問題は本文にある選択肢を一つずつ消去していくのがコツです。
