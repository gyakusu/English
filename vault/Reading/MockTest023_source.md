## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Notice)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**NOTICE TO ALL TENANTS — Scheduled Elevator Maintenance**

Harborview Tower Management Office
Posted: April 20, 2026

Please be advised that our elevator service provider, LiftPro Solutions, will conduct annual inspections and mechanical upgrades on the passenger elevators serving floors 10 through 25. The work will take place from **Monday, May 4 through Friday, May 8, 2026**, between the hours of 9:00 P.M. and 5:00 A.M.

During these overnight hours, Elevators A and B will be completely out of service. Elevator C, located in the north lobby, will remain operational at all times and should be used for any late-night access. The freight elevator may also be used upon request; please call the security desk in advance to arrange assistance.

We recognize that this work may cause minor inconvenience and sincerely appreciate your patience. The upgrades are expected to improve travel speed and energy efficiency by approximately fifteen percent.

For questions or special access requests, please contact Mr. Daniel Ortiz, Building Operations Manager, at 555-0142 or d.ortiz@harborviewtower.com.

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the notice?**
(A) To announce a change in the building's operating hours
(B) To inform tenants about upcoming elevator maintenance
(C) To introduce a new elevator service provider
(D) To request feedback on recent building renovations

**2. According to the notice, what should tenants do if they need to use the freight elevator?**
(A) Submit a written request to the management office
(B) Wait until the maintenance period has ended
(C) Contact the security desk beforehand
(D) Email Mr. Ortiz directly

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** タイトル "NOTICE TO ALL TENANTS — Scheduled Elevator Maintenance" と本文冒頭 "will conduct annual inspections and mechanical upgrades on the passenger elevators" から、エレベーター保守作業を入居者に知らせる通知であると分かる。(A) 営業時間の変更ではなく夜間作業の案内。(C) LiftPro Solutions は既存の業者で「新しく紹介する」わけではない。(D) フィードバック募集ではない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文中 "The freight elevator may also be used upon request; please call the security desk in advance to arrange assistance." を根拠とする。"call the security desk in advance" が選択肢 (C) の "Contact the security desk beforehand" に言い換えられている (in advance → beforehand)。(A) 書面での申請は言及なし。(B) 期間終了まで待つ必要はない。(D) Ortiz 氏は一般的な問い合わせ窓口で、貨物用エレベーター利用の連絡先ではない。
