## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Text Message Chain)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Ravi Desai (9:42 A.M.):**
Hi Maya — quick question. Did the revised contract from Fenwick & Co. arrive yet? Legal wants to review it before tomorrow's call.

**Maya Lindgren (9:44 A.M.):**
Not yet. Their assistant said she'd send it by end of day yesterday, but I haven't seen anything in my inbox.

**Ravi Desai (9:45 A.M.):**
That's frustrating. We can't move forward without it. Can you follow up this morning?

**Maya Lindgren (9:47 A.M.):**
Will do. I'll also cc Fenwick's partner directly so it doesn't get lost at the assistant level again. Should I tell them we need it by noon?

**Ravi Desai (9:48 A.M.):**
Yes — noon at the latest. And if it doesn't arrive, I'll need you to let Legal know so they can reschedule the review session.

**Maya Lindgren (9:49 A.M.):**
Understood. I'll keep you posted.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What does Mr. Desai ask Ms. Lindgren to do?**
(A) Draft a new version of the contract
(B) Cancel the call scheduled for tomorrow
(C) Contact Fenwick & Co. about a missing document
(D) Attend the legal review session in person

**2. At 9:47 A.M., what does Ms. Lindgren most likely mean when she writes, "I'll also cc Fenwick's partner directly"?**
(A) She wants to thank the partner for their cooperation
(B) She believes contacting a higher-level person will get a faster response
(C) She intends to forward the assistant's previous e-mail
(D) She has been asked to keep Legal off the communication

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Mr. Desai は "Can you follow up this morning?" と依頼しており、これは契約書が届いていないFenwick & Co.への連絡を意味します。"missing document" ＝ 届いていない契約書の言い換え。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 直前に "it doesn't get lost at the assistant level again"（アシスタントレベルでまた埋もれないように）とあります。つまりアシスタントより上位のパートナーに直接CCすることで対応を早めたい意図です。(A)(C)(D)は文脈に合いません。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Revised:** 修正された、改訂された
> - **Follow up:** フォローアップする、追跡確認する
> - **At the latest:** 遅くとも
> - **Cc (someone):** 〜をCCに入れる
> - **Get lost (in email):** 埋もれる、見落とされる
> - **Keep (someone) posted:** 逐次報告する
