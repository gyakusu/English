## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Product Recall Notice)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE — VOLUNTARY PRODUCT RECALL**
**Issued by:** Brightway Appliances, Inc.
**Date:** April 16, 2026

Brightway Appliances is voluntarily recalling all units of its **AeroBlend 900 countertop blender** manufactured between September 2025 and February 2026. Testing has revealed that, under certain conditions, the motor housing may overheat, posing a potential burn hazard to users.

**How to identify affected units:**
Check the serial number on the base of the blender. Units beginning with "AB9-2509" through "AB9-2602" are affected. If your serial number falls outside this range, no action is required.

**What to do:**
1. Stop using the product immediately.
2. Visit www.brightway.com/recall to register your unit.
3. You will receive a free replacement blender within 14 business days. There is no need to return the original unit — please dispose of it in accordance with local electronics recycling guidelines.

No injuries have been reported to date, but consumer safety is our highest priority. For questions, please call our dedicated recall helpline at 1-800-555-0042, available seven days a week.

### [Questions]

**1. What potential risk is associated with the recalled product?**
(A) The motor housing may become dangerously hot
(B) The blades may detach during operation
(C) The power cord may cause electrical shocks
(D) The container may crack under pressure

**2. What are affected customers advised to do with their original unit?**
(A) Return it to the retailer where it was purchased
(B) Mail it back to Brightway Appliances
(C) Hold onto it until a replacement is received
(D) Dispose of it through local recycling services

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** 第1段落に "the motor housing may overheat, posing a potential burn hazard" とあります。"overheat" ＝ 過熱する、"become dangerously hot" と一致。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** 手順3に "please dispose of it in accordance with local electronics recycling guidelines" とあります。"dispose of through local recycling services" と一致。(B)の返送は "There is no need to return the original unit" と明記されており逆の内容。
