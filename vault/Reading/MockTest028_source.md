## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Report)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**MEMORANDUM**

**To:** All Store Managers
**From:** Daniela Ortiz, Customer Experience Division
**Date:** April 15, 2026
**Subject:** Q1 Customer Satisfaction Survey Results

The Customer Experience Division has completed its review of the first-quarter customer satisfaction survey, conducted between January and March 2026. A total of 2,480 shoppers across our 34 Brightmark retail locations submitted responses, a 12% increase over the same period last year.

Overall satisfaction reached 87%, the highest score recorded since the survey was launched in 2022. Respondents rated **store cleanliness** most favorably (92%), followed by **staff friendliness** (90%). However, **checkout wait time** received our lowest rating at just 68%, with many customers noting that lines during weekend evenings were unreasonably long.

In response, the division will launch a pilot program at six flagship stores next month, introducing two additional self-checkout kiosks and revised weekend staffing schedules. A follow-up survey will be distributed in July to measure the impact of these changes. Managers are asked to share these results with their teams by April 30.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the memo?**
(A) To announce the launch of a new customer survey
(B) To report the findings of a recent satisfaction survey
(C) To introduce a new division within the company
(D) To request feedback from store managers

**2. According to the memo, what issue was most frequently raised by customers?**
(A) The cleanliness of the stores
(B) The attitude of the staff
(C) The length of checkout lines
(D) The limited weekend hours

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** メモの冒頭で "The Customer Experience Division has completed its review of the first-quarter customer satisfaction survey" と述べられ、以降は調査結果(満足度87%、項目別の高評価・低評価)を報告している。したがって主目的は「最近の満足度調査の結果を報告すること」であり (B) が正解。(A) は調査は既に実施済みなので不可。(C) 部門の紹介ではなく、(D) 管理職にフィードバックを求めているわけでもない(結果の共有を依頼しているだけ)。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文中 "checkout wait time received our lowest rating at just 68%, with many customers noting that lines during weekend evenings were unreasonably long" とあり、レジ待ち時間、特に週末夕方の行列が最も多く指摘された問題である。よって (C) が正解。(A) cleanliness と (B) staff friendliness はむしろ高評価の項目。(D) weekend hours(営業時間)には言及がない。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **satisfaction survey:** 満足度調査(例: conduct a satisfaction survey = 満足度調査を実施する)
> - **respondent:** 回答者(surveyの答えを返した人)
> - **favorably:** 好意的に、良い評価で(↔ unfavorably)
> - **checkout wait time:** レジでの待ち時間
> - **unreasonably:** 不当に、理不尽なほどに
> - **pilot program:** 試験的プログラム、パイロット事業
> - **flagship store:** 旗艦店(ブランドを代表する主要店舗)
> - **follow-up survey:** 追跡調査、フォローアップ調査
