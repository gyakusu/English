## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Letter)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Gleneagle Mutual Insurance Co.**
227 Kingsford Road, Edinburgh EH1 3AA
April 8, 2026

Mr. Thomas Brennan
14 Harbour Lane
Edinburgh EH6 4AP

**Re: Renewal of Home Insurance Policy No. HM-448219**

Dear Mr. Brennan,

Your current home insurance policy is scheduled to expire on May 31, 2026. We would like to thank you for being a valued customer of Gleneagle Mutual for the past seven years and invite you to renew your coverage for another twelve months.

Based on your claims history and the modest increase in regional property values, we are pleased to offer a renewal premium of **£412.80**, which represents only a 1.5% increase from the previous year. This is considerably below the industry average for comparable policies this year.

If you would like to renew at these terms, no further action is required — the renewal will process automatically on May 15. However, if you wish to make any adjustments to your coverage, update your contact details, or discuss optional add-ons (such as accidental damage or home-office equipment cover), please call us at 0131 555 0912 before May 10.

We appreciate your continued trust in our services.

Yours sincerely,
Fiona McAllister
Personal Lines Manager

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is Mr. Brennan NOT required to do if he is satisfied with the renewal terms?**
(A) Pay the new premium of £412.80
(B) Continue paying for his home insurance coverage
(C) Take any action before the policy renews
(D) Notify Gleneagle Mutual of his decision to renew

**2. Why does the letter highlight the 1.5% increase?**
(A) To show that the increase is smaller than industry norms
(B) To warn Mr. Brennan of rising premium costs
(C) To justify a change in the terms of coverage
(D) To encourage him to switch to a different policy

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第3段落に "no further action is required — the renewal will process automatically on May 15" とあります。満足していれば何も行動しなくてよい、が正解。(A)(B)は更新後当然発生する義務、(D)も "no further action" に含まれるので別の選択肢として成り立ちますが、設問の問いは「更新時に何をする必要がないか」で、"Take any action before the policy renews" が最も直接的な言い換え（"no further action" ＝ "any action" の否定）です。
>
> **2. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** "This is considerably below the industry average for comparable policies this year" とあります。つまり1.5%の値上げは業界平均より大幅に低いとアピールするためです。
