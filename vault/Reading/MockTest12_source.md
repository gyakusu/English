## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (News Article)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Local Bakery Chain Announces Regional Expansion**
*By Lorena Castillo, Metro Business Daily — April 14, 2026*

Harvest Loaf, a family-owned artisan bakery chain founded in Portland twelve years ago, announced yesterday that it will open ten new locations across the Pacific Northwest by the end of 2027. The expansion represents the company's largest investment to date and will create approximately 180 jobs in the region.

Founder and CEO Margaret Alvarez cited rising demand for locally sourced baked goods as the principal driver behind the decision. "Our customers have consistently told us they want Harvest Loaf closer to home," Alvarez said at a press briefing. "This expansion is our response to that demand."

Industry analysts note that the move comes at a time when many national bakery chains are scaling back. However, Alvarez remains confident that Harvest Loaf's commitment to local ingredients and small-batch production will continue to distinguish the brand. The first of the new locations is expected to open in Eugene, Oregon, by late August.

---

### [Questions]

**1. According to Ms. Alvarez, what is the main reason for the expansion?**
(A) A recent increase in the company's advertising budget
(B) Strong customer demand for more convenient locations
(C) The closure of competitors in the Pacific Northwest
(D) A merger with a larger national bakery brand

**2. What is suggested about the bakery industry overall?**
(A) It has recently been expanding faster than any other sector
(B) It relies heavily on imported ingredients
(C) Many large chains are currently reducing their operations
(D) It is dominated by companies based in Oregon

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 第2段落で CEO が "Our customers have consistently told us they want Harvest Loaf closer to home" と述べており、顧客からの「近くに店を」という要望に応えた拡大だと分かります。"customer demand for more convenient locations" はこの言い換え。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第3段落に "the move comes at a time when many national bakery chains are scaling back" とあります。"scaling back" ＝ 規模を縮小する、"reducing their operations" と同義。(A)はむしろ逆の内容です。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Artisan:** 職人の、手工芸の
> - **To date:** これまでに
> - **Cite (something) as:** 〜を理由として挙げる
> - **Locally sourced:** 地元産の
> - **Press briefing:** 記者会見
> - **Scale back:** 規模を縮小する
> - **Small-batch:** 少量生産の
> - **Distinguish:** 区別する、差別化する
