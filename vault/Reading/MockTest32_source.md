## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Advertisement)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Learn to Cook Like a Pro at Maple Leaf Culinary Academy!**

Maple Leaf Culinary Academy is pleased to announce its popular **Weekend Gourmet Series**, a six-week hands-on cooking program designed for home cooks who want to take their skills to the next level. Classes will be held every Saturday from **May 9 to June 13, 2026**, at our newly renovated teaching kitchen in downtown Vancouver.

Each three-hour session is led by **Chef Marco Bellini**, a graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris with over 15 years of experience in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe.

Weekly topics include:
- Classic French sauces
- Fresh handmade pasta
- Japanese knife skills
- Artisan bread baking
- Plant-based main courses
- Elegant dessert plating

The full six-week series is priced at **$540 per person**, including all ingredients and a recipe booklet. Register before **April 30** with code **EARLY15** to receive a 15% early-bird discount. Groups of three or more receive an additional 10% off.

Seats are limited. Visit **www.mapleleafculinary.ca** or call **(604) 555-0182** to reserve your spot today.

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the advertisement?**
(A) To announce the grand opening of a restaurant
(B) To promote a series of cooking classes
(C) To recruit professional chefs for a culinary school
(D) To introduce a new line of kitchen products

**2. What is NOT mentioned as being included with the program?**
(A) Ingredients for the classes
(B) Instruction from an experienced chef
(C) A set of professional knives
(D) A booklet containing recipes

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 広告の冒頭で "Maple Leaf Culinary Academy is pleased to announce its popular Weekend Gourmet Series, a six-week hands-on cooking program" と述べており、全体を通じて料理教室シリーズの宣伝であることがわかります。したがって (B) が正解です。(A) レストランの開店、(C) シェフの募集、(D) 調理器具の販売はいずれも本文に該当する記述がありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文には "priced at $540 per person, including all ingredients and a recipe booklet" とあり、(A) ingredients と (D) recipe booklet が含まれると明記されています。また (B) については "led by Chef Marco Bellini, a graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris with over 15 years of experience" と経験豊富なシェフの指導が述べられています。一方、(C) のプロ用ナイフのセットについては言及がないため、NOT mentioned の答えは (C) です。
