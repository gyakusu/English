## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Article)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Greenfield Manufacturing Unveils Ambitious Sustainability Roadmap**
*By Martha Collins | Business Weekly | March 14, 2026*

Greenfield Manufacturing, a mid-sized producer of industrial packaging based in Portland, Oregon, announced last Tuesday that it has launched a comprehensive environmental initiative known as "Project Horizon." Founded in 1987, the company employs nearly 1,200 workers across four facilities in the Pacific Northwest.

As part of the program, Greenfield has installed more than 3,400 solar panels on the rooftops of its two largest plants, which are expected to supply roughly 45 percent of the firm's electricity needs. The company has also overhauled its internal recycling systems, reducing landfill waste by 62 percent compared to 2023 levels.

"Our customers increasingly expect responsibility, not just quality," said CEO Daniel Rourke during a press briefing. "Project Horizon reflects the values our employees have championed for years."

Looking ahead, Greenfield aims to achieve carbon neutrality by 2032 and plans to convert its delivery fleet entirely to electric vehicles within the next five years. Industry analysts have praised the roadmap as both practical and measurable.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the article?**
(A) To announce the opening of a new manufacturing plant
(B) To report on a company's new environmental program
(C) To criticize a firm's waste management practices
(D) To compare several packaging manufacturers

**2. What is NOT mentioned as part of Project Horizon?**
(A) Installation of solar panels
(B) Improvements to recycling systems
(C) Donations to environmental charities
(D) Plans for an electric delivery fleet

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 記事冒頭で "Greenfield Manufacturing ... announced last Tuesday that it has launched a comprehensive environmental initiative known as 'Project Horizon.'" とあり、環境プログラムの発表が記事の主目的。よって (B) が正解。新工場開設 (A) や批判 (C)、比較記事 (D) ではない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文では "installed more than 3,400 solar panels"(A)、"overhauled its internal recycling systems"(B)、"convert its delivery fleet entirely to electric vehicles"(D) が述べられているが、慈善団体への寄付については一切言及がない。したがって (C) が正解。

### 🔑 重要語彙

> - **initiative:** 新たな取り組み、戦略
> - **overhaul:** ~を全面的に見直す、刷新する
