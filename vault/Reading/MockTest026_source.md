## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Article)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Riverton Startup Secures Major Funding for Expansion**
*By Megan Holloway — March 14, 2026*

RIVERTON — Brightloop Technologies, a local software company specializing in logistics management tools, announced yesterday that it has secured 42 million dollars in Series B funding. The round was led by Carlton Ventures, with additional contributions from several regional investors.

Founded in 2019, Brightloop currently employs 85 people at its downtown headquarters. CEO Daniel Ueda stated, "This investment allows us to accelerate product development and extend our reach to clients across North America." The company plans to open a second office in Austin, Texas, by the end of the year and hire approximately 60 new engineers over the next 18 months.

Brightloop's flagship platform helps small and mid-sized retailers optimize their delivery routes, reportedly reducing transportation costs by up to 22 percent. Industry analyst Karen Liu noted that the logistics software market is expected to grow significantly as e-commerce demand continues to rise.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the article?**
(A) To review a new logistics software product
(B) To report on a company's recent funding success
(C) To announce the opening of a new headquarters
(D) To compare several technology startups

**2. What does Brightloop NOT plan to do with the new funding?**
(A) Open an office in Austin
(B) Hire additional engineers
(C) Acquire a competing company
(D) Accelerate product development

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 記事冒頭で "Brightloop Technologies ... announced yesterday that it has secured 42 million dollars in Series B funding" と述べられており、地元企業の資金調達成功を伝えることが記事の主な目的。(A)は製品レビューではなく、(C)は「second office」の話で headquarters 開設ではない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文には "open a second office in Austin, Texas"(A)、"hire approximately 60 new engineers"(B)、"accelerate product development"(D) と明記されているが、競合他社の買収(acquire a competing company)については一切触れられていない。NOT 問題は本文にある選択肢を消去していくのがコツ。
