## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Letter)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

Riverton Chamber of Commerce
48 Market Street, Riverton

April 21, 2026

Dr. Helena Moreau
Moreau Research Institute
122 Linden Avenue, Greendale

Dear Dr. Moreau,

On behalf of the entire membership of the Riverton Chamber of Commerce, I would like to express our sincere gratitude for your outstanding keynote address at our Annual Business Gala, held on April 10 at the Grand Harbor Hotel. Your presentation, "Sustainable Growth for Regional Economies," was by far the most talked-about portion of the evening.

Several attendees have since contacted our office to say that your case studies on small-town revitalization inspired them to reexamine their own business strategies. A number of local shop owners are already drafting proposals based on the cooperative models you introduced.

As a small token of our appreciation, please accept the enclosed gift card to The Ivy Bookshop, along with the agreed honorarium, which has been transferred to your institute this morning.

We would be honored to welcome you back next year, perhaps for a panel discussion. I will contact your assistant in the autumn to explore the possibility.

With warm regards,

Martin Caldwell
Chairperson, Riverton Chamber of Commerce

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the letter?**
(A) To invite Dr. Moreau to a new event next month
(B) To thank Dr. Moreau for delivering a keynote speech
(C) To request a written copy of Dr. Moreau's speech
(D) To confirm the payment schedule for a consulting project

**2. What is indicated about the gala attendees?**
(A) They were mostly researchers from the Moreau Institute.
(B) They voted Dr. Moreau the best speaker of the year.
(C) Some have begun applying ideas from the speech to their businesses.
(D) They requested a discount on future Chamber memberships.

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 冒頭で "I would like to express our sincere gratitude for your outstanding keynote address at our Annual Business Gala" と述べられており、年次ガラでの基調講演に対する感謝を伝えるのが手紙の主目的。よって (B) が正解。(A) 来年の招待には触れているが「来月」ではない。(C) 講演原稿の要求は書かれていない。(D) 謝礼金は既に送金済みと述べられているだけで、支払スケジュールの確認ではない。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "A number of local shop owners are already drafting proposals based on the cooperative models you introduced." とあり、講演で紹介された協同モデルを基に自らのビジネスに活用し始めている参加者がいることが分かる。したがって (C) が正解。(A)(B)(D) はいずれも本文に根拠がない。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示

> - **sincere gratitude:** 心からの感謝
> - **keynote address:** 基調講演
> - **attendee:** 出席者、参加者
> - **revitalization:** 再活性化、活性化
> - **cooperative model:** 協同組合型モデル、協業モデル
> - **honorarium:** 謝礼金
> - **panel discussion:** パネルディスカッション、公開討論会
