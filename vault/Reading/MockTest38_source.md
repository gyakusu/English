## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Announcement)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Golden Crust Bakery — Grand Opening Announcement**

Golden Crust Bakery is delighted to announce the opening of our newest branch in downtown Portland. After serving the Pacific Northwest for over 25 years, we are proud to bring our award-winning sourdough bread, pastries, and specialty cakes to a brand-new neighborhood.

Our new location at 418 Maple Street will officially open its doors on **Saturday, May 9, 2026**. Regular business hours will be 7:00 A.M. to 8:00 P.M., Monday through Saturday, and 8:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M. on Sundays.

To celebrate this special occasion, we will be holding a ribbon-cutting ceremony at 9:30 A.M., featuring Mayor Linda Chen. The first 100 customers will receive a complimentary loaf of our signature honey walnut bread. Throughout opening day, all croissants and muffins will be offered at 30% off.

Customers who present the code **GCB2026** at the register will also receive a free reusable tote bag while supplies last. For more information, visit www.goldencrustbakery.com or call (503) 555-0142.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the announcement?**
(A) To promote a seasonal menu change
(B) To invite customers to a charity event
(C) To notify the public about a new store location
(D) To recruit staff for an existing bakery

**2. What will happen at 9:30 A.M. on opening day?**
(A) The first 100 customers will be counted.
(B) A ribbon-cutting ceremony will take place.
(C) Discounted croissants will be sold out.
(D) The bakery's website will be launched.

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文冒頭で "Golden Crust Bakery is delighted to announce the opening of our newest branch in downtown Portland." と述べられており、新店舗オープンの告知がこのアナウンスの主目的です。さらに "Our new location at 418 Maple Street will officially open its doors on Saturday, May 9, 2026." と具体的な住所とオープン日が続くことからも、(C) To notify the public about a new store location が正解と判断できます。(A)(B)(D) はいずれも本文で触れられていません。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 本文に "we will be holding a ribbon-cutting ceremony at 9:30 A.M., featuring Mayor Linda Chen." と明記されています。午前9時30分に行われるイベントは「リボンカットの式典」なので (B) A ribbon-cutting ceremony will take place. が正解。(A) の「100人の客を数える」は時刻が特定されておらず誤り。(C) は croissants は終日30%オフと書かれているだけで「売り切れる」とは述べられていません。(D) はウェブサイトは既に存在し "visit www.goldencrustbakery.com" と案内されているだけです。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **branch:** 支店、支社
> - **award-winning:** 受賞歴のある、賞を獲得した
> - **ribbon-cutting ceremony:** テープカット(開店・開通)式
> - **complimentary:** 無料の、サービスの

> - **while supplies last:** 在庫がなくなり次第(終了)
> - **register (n.):** レジ、レジスター
> - **reusable tote bag:** 再利用可能なトートバッグ
