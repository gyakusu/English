## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Internal Announcement)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**INTERNAL ANNOUNCEMENT**
**From:** Patricia Lam, Chief Executive Officer
**To:** All Staff — Wexford Analytics
**Date:** April 17, 2026
**Subject:** Appointment of New Chief Technology Officer

I am delighted to announce that Dr. Hiroshi Nakamura will be joining Wexford Analytics as our new Chief Technology Officer, effective May 12. Dr. Nakamura brings over fifteen years of leadership experience in data infrastructure, most recently as Vice President of Engineering at Meridian Data Systems.

In his new role, Dr. Nakamura will oversee our cloud migration initiative and lead the expansion of our machine-learning platform. He will report directly to me and will be based in our London headquarters, though he plans to visit our regional offices during his first quarter.

A welcome reception will be held in the main atrium on May 15 at 4:00 P.M. All employees are warmly invited to attend and meet Dr. Nakamura in person. Light refreshments will be provided.

Please join me in extending a warm welcome to Dr. Nakamura.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is one of Dr. Nakamura's primary responsibilities in his new position?**
(A) Managing international sales operations
(B) Leading the company's cloud migration project
(C) Recruiting staff for the London headquarters
(D) Preparing quarterly financial statements

**2. What is stated about the welcome reception?**
(A) It is restricted to senior management only
(B) A formal dinner will be served
(C) Attendance must be confirmed in advance
(D) It is open to all employees of the company

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "Dr. Nakamura will oversee our cloud migration initiative" とあります。"oversee" ＝ 監督する、"lead" と同義。(A)(C)(D)はいずれも記載なし。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** 第3段落に "All employees are warmly invited to attend" とあります。"All employees" が "open to all employees" と一致。(B)は "Light refreshments"（軽食）であり、正式なディナーではありません。(C)は事前確認について記載がありません。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Appointment:** 任命、就任
> - **Effective (date):** 〜付けで
> - **Oversee:** 監督する、統括する
> - **Initiative:** 取り組み、新計画
> - **Atrium:** アトリウム、吹き抜けのあるロビー
> - **Refreshments:** 軽食、飲み物
> - **Extend a welcome:** 歓迎の意を伝える
