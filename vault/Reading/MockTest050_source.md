## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Notice)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Riverside Food Truck Festival — Save the Date!**

The Downtown Business Alliance is thrilled to announce the return of the Riverside Food Truck Festival, taking place at Centennial Park on **Saturday, June 13 and Sunday, June 14, 2026**, from 11:00 A.M. to 9:00 P.M. both days.

This year's festival will feature more than **30 gourmet food trucks** offering a wide variety of cuisines, from classic American barbecue and wood-fired pizza to Korean tacos, Vietnamese banh mi, and vegan desserts. Local craft beverage vendors will also be on site.

Enjoy live entertainment on two outdoor stages, including jazz bands on Saturday afternoon and a popular local rock group headlining Sunday evening. A dedicated Family Zone will offer face painting and games for children under twelve.

**Admission is free.** On-site parking is limited, so visitors are strongly encouraged to use public transit; the Green Line stops just two blocks from the park entrance. A portion of all vendor proceeds will be donated to the Riverside Youth Literacy Program.

For the full schedule and vendor list, please visit **www.riversidefoodtrucks.org** or call (555) 204-7788.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is indicated about the festival?**
(A) It is being held for the first time this year.
(B) It will take place over two consecutive days.
(C) It requires attendees to purchase tickets in advance.
(D) It will be held indoors in case of rain.

**2. How are visitors encouraged to travel to the event?**
(A) By carpooling with neighbors
(B) By parking in a nearby garage
(C) By taking public transportation
(D) By riding a shuttle bus from downtown

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 本文に "taking place at Centennial Park on **Saturday, June 13 and Sunday, June 14, 2026**" とあり、土曜と日曜の連続した2日間にわたり開催されることが分かります。よって (B) "It will take place over two consecutive days." が正解です。(A) は "the return of the Riverside Food Truck Festival" (再開催) とあるので誤り。(C) は "Admission is free." と矛盾。(D) については屋内開催の記述はありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 本文に "visitors are strongly encouraged to use public transit" と明記されており、さらに "the Green Line stops just two blocks from the park entrance" と公共交通機関の具体例が示されています。よって (C) "By taking public transportation" が正解。(B) は "On-site parking is limited" とあり推奨されていません。carpooling (相乗り) や shuttle bus の言及は本文にありません。
