## 📝 Part 7: Double Passage (Itinerary + E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 5 mins

### [Passage 1: Travel Itinerary]

**Business Trip Itinerary — Carla Whitmore**
**Destination:** Singapore
**Prepared by:** Harbour Business Travel

| Date | Time | Activity |
|------|------|----------|
| May 6 (Mon) | 14:30 | Depart London Heathrow (SQ 317) |
| May 7 (Tue) | 09:55 | Arrive Singapore Changi |
| May 7 (Tue) | 14:00 | Client meeting — Alto Pacific Logistics |
| May 8 (Wed) | 10:00 | Site tour — Jurong distribution centre |
| May 8 (Wed) | 19:00 | Dinner with regional managers |
| May 9 (Thu) | 11:30 | Depart Singapore (SQ 318) |

All airport transfers have been arranged. Hotel: The Orchid Suites, May 7–8.

---

### [Passage 2: E-mail]

**From:** Carla Whitmore
**To:** Steven Park, Harbour Business Travel
**Date:** April 28
**Subject:** Urgent — Itinerary adjustment required

Dear Steven,

Thank you for the itinerary. I need to request one adjustment. The client meeting on May 7 has been pushed forward to the morning of May 8, which means the original site tour at the Jurong distribution centre will need to be moved.

Could you coordinate with the Jurong team to reschedule the site tour to the afternoon of May 8? If that is not possible, I would prefer to cancel the site tour altogether rather than miss the client meeting. The evening dinner with regional managers should remain as scheduled — I have already confirmed with them.

Also, please extend my hotel booking by one additional night, as the schedule change will likely require me to stay through the evening of May 9 and depart on May 10 instead.

Many thanks,
Carla

---

### [Questions]

**1. What change to the itinerary does Ms. Whitmore request?**
(A) Moving her departure flight to an earlier time
(B) Cancelling the dinner with regional managers
(C) Replacing her hotel with one closer to Jurong
(D) Rescheduling a site visit to later the same day

**2. What is implied about the evening dinner on May 8?**
(A) It will take place as originally planned
(B) It will be postponed to the following evening
(C) It will be cancelled due to the schedule conflict
(D) It will be combined with the client meeting

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** Passage 2 に "Could you coordinate with the Jurong team to reschedule the site tour to the afternoon of May 8?" とあります。Passage 1 では site tour は5月8日の午前10時ですが、これを同日の午後に変更したいという依頼です。"later the same day" ＝ 同日の遅い時間、と一致します。
>
> **2. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** Passage 2 に "The evening dinner with regional managers should remain as scheduled — I have already confirmed with them" とあります。"remain as scheduled" ＝ 予定通り行われる、と一致。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Itinerary:** 旅程表
> - **Depart / Arrive:** 出発する／到着する
> - **Transfer:** 送迎、移動手段
> - **Push forward:** 前倒しする
> - **Coordinate with:** 〜と調整する
> - **Reschedule:** 予定を変更する
> - **Altogether:** 完全に、全部で
> - **Remain as scheduled:** 予定通り行われる

### 🧠 学習のポイント
> [!tip]- ダブルパッセージのコツ
> 時刻表・予定表（Passage 1）と、その変更依頼メール（Passage 2）を照合する典型パターンです。
> 1. **日付と時間の照合**: Passage 1の「May 8 10:00 site tour」とPassage 2の「reschedule the site tour to the afternoon」を突き合わせる。
> 2. **変更されない項目の確認**: "remain as scheduled" という表現に注目。選択肢の(A)(B)(C)を吟味するときに大きなヒントになります。
