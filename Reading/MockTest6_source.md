## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**From:** Michael Torres, Purchasing Manager
**To:** Linda Yao, Sales Representative, Orion Office Supplies
**Date:** April 18, 2026
**Subject:** Follow-up on Bulk Order Quotation

Dear Ms. Yao,

Thank you for sending over the quotation for 500 units of ergonomic desk chairs (Model EX-7). We reviewed it carefully and appreciate the competitive pricing.

However, our finance team requires that any order over $10,000 include an itemized invoice, a projected delivery schedule, and documentation confirming compliance with our company's supplier sustainability standards.

Could you please provide these documents at your earliest convenience? Once we receive them, we expect the approval process to take no more than five business days. We look forward to finalizing the order.

Best regards,
Michael Torres

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of Mr. Torres's e-mail?**
(A) To request additional documents before completing a purchase
(B) To complain about the quality of a previously delivered product
(C) To cancel an existing order due to a budget reduction
(D) To inquire about the availability of a new product line

**2. What does Mr. Torres indicate will happen after the documents are received?**
(A) The chairs will be shipped within five business days
(B) A revised quotation will be issued by the finance team
(C) The order approval process will begin
(D) A meeting with senior management will be arranged

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** メールの要点は "Could you please provide these documents"（書類を提供してほしい）という依頼です。購入を完了する前に必要書類を要求しているため(A)が正解。(C)(D)は本文に一切記載がありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** "Once we receive them, we expect the approval process to take no more than five business days" とあります。書類受領後に承認プロセス（approval process）が始まるということです。(A)は "chairs will be shipped" ではなく "approval process" が5営業日かかるという内容の混同です。
