### Part 7: Double Passage — Mock Test 2
**Time Goal:** 8 mins (5 questions)

---

#### [Passage 1: Advertisement]
**State-of-the-Art Office Space Available at The Meridian Tower**

Are you looking to elevate your business profile? The Meridian Tower offers premium office suites in the heart of the financial district.

**Features include:**
* High-speed fiber-optic internet
* 24/7 security and concierge service
* Floor-to-ceiling windows with panoramic city views
* Access to our exclusive rooftop lounge and fitness center

**Special Offer:** Sign a 24-month lease by June 1st and receive the first two months rent-free! Additionally, tenants signing this quarter will have the standard security deposit reduced by 50%.

Contact Julian Varga at `j.varga@meridiantower.com` for a private tour.

---

#### [Passage 2: E-mail]
**From:** Elena Rossi, CEO of Nexa Design Studios  
**To:** Julian Varga  
**Date:** May 12  
**Subject:** Inquiry regarding office suites

Dear Mr. Varga,

I am writing regarding the advertisement for office space at The Meridian Tower. Our current lease at the North Plaza expires in late July, and we are looking for a more modern space that reflects our company's growing reputation in the design industry.

We are very interested in the premium suites, specifically those on the upper floors. However, our budget is somewhat constrained by a major project expansion we are currently undertaking. The rent-free period mentioned in your ad is very appealing. Could you clarify if the reduced security deposit applies even if we opt for a shorter lease term, such as 12 months?

We would like to schedule a tour for next Tuesday afternoon. Please let me know if that works for you.

Sincerely,

Elena Rossi

---

#### [Questions]

**1. What is suggested about Nexa Design Studios?**
(A) It is currently facing financial difficulties due to a failed project.
(B) It plans to relocate its office before the end of July.
(C) It has more employees than its current office can accommodate.
(D) It is a startup company looking for its first office space.

**2. Which incentive is Ms. Rossi NOT certain she qualifies for?**
(A) The two months of free rent
(B) The high-speed fiber-optic internet access
(C) The reduction in the security deposit
(D) The access to the rooftop fitness center

**3. What is the main purpose of Ms. Rossi's e-mail?**
(A) To inform Mr. Varga that Nexa Design Studios has decided to sign a lease
(B) To request a tour and ask about lease conditions for a shorter term
(C) To negotiate a lower monthly rent for the upper-floor suites
(D) To report an error in the advertised special offer

**4. What is NOT listed as an amenity of The Meridian Tower?**
(A) A rooftop lounge
(B) A fitness center
(C) Underground parking
(D) 24/7 concierge service

**5. According to the advertisement, what condition must be met to receive the rent-free period?**
(A) The tenant must pay the full security deposit in advance.
(B) The lease must be signed before June 1st for a term of at least 24 months.
(C) The tenant must first schedule a private tour with Julian Varga.
(D) The tenant must be signing a lease for the first time this quarter.

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
>
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Passage 2の "Our current lease at the North Plaza expires in late July" より、現在の契約が7月末に切れるため7月が終わる前に移転する計画が示唆されています。(A)(C)(D) は本文に根拠なし。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 敷金50%減額の条件は "tenants signing this quarter"（今四半期中に契約）です。Ms. Rossiは12ヶ月契約でも適用されるか質問しており、資格があるか不明です。2ヶ月無料（A）は24ヶ月契約が条件と広告に明記されており、彼女はその条件を知った上で "very appealing" と述べています。
>
> **3. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Eメールの目的は「ツアーを来週火曜午後に予約したい」という依頼と、「12ヶ月契約でも敷金減額が適用されるか」という条件確認の2点です。契約締結の通知（A）でも、賃料交渉（C）でも、広告の誤り報告（D）でもありません。
>
> **4. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Passage 1に記載されている設備はHigh-speed internet / 24/7 security & concierge / panoramic windows / rooftop lounge & fitness centerの4つです。Underground parking（地下駐車場）は一切言及されていません。
>
> **5. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** "Sign a 24-month lease by June 1st and receive the first two months rent-free" と明記されています。24ヶ月契約かつ6月1日までの締結が条件です。(D)の「初めての契約」という条件は存在しません。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Elevate:** 高める、向上させる
> - **Panoramic:** 全景の、パノラマの
> - **Concierge:** コンシェルジュ（受付・案内係）
> - **Lease:** 賃貸借契約
> - **Security deposit:** 敷金、保証金
> - **Expire:** 期限が切れる、満了する
> - **Constrained:** 制約された、不自由な
> - **Inquiry:** 問い合わせ
> - **Amenity:** 設備、アメニティ
> - **Opt for:** ～を選ぶ

### 🧠 学習のポイント
> [!tip]- ポイント解説
> 1. **NOT問題（Q2・Q4）:** 選択肢を1つずつ本文と照合する。本文に登場しないものが正解。
> 2. **目的問題（Q3）:** メールの冒頭や末尾に目的が集中する。"I am writing regarding..." と最終段落の依頼文を確認。
> 3. **条件の読み取り（Q5）:** 特典には必ず条件がある。数字（24ヶ月、June 1st）と条件語（must, by, if）に注意。
> 4. **Cross-reference（Q1・Q2）:** 2つのパッセージをまたいで情報を照合するのがDouble Passageの核心。
