**RapidShelf — Smart Storage for Modern Homes**

Tired of cluttered rooms and wasted space? RapidShelf's modular shelving systems are designed to fit any room, any lifestyle.

**Why choose RapidShelf?**
- Fully customizable configurations

- Premium steel and bamboo construction

Free shipping on all orders over $100. Visit www.rapidshelf.com or call 1-800-555-0199.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is offered free of charge to customers who spend $150 or more?**
(A) A complimentary toolkit
(B) Next-day delivery service
(C) A 20% discount on the total order
(D) A one-year extended warranty

**2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a feature of RapidShelf products?**
(A) They are customizable in configuration
(B) They are made from premium materials
(C) They require professional installation
(D) They come with a manufacturer's warranty

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (A)**
> **解説:** "receive a FREE RapidShelf toolkit (valued at $29.99)" とあります。"complimentary" は "free" の言い換えです。(B)の無料配送は$100以上が条件であり、$150以上のプレゼントとは別の特典です。
>
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 広告には "No tools required — assembles in minutes"（工具不要、数分で組み立て可）とあり、プロによる設置が必要とは一切書かれていません。(A)(B)(D)はいずれも本文に記載があります。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Cluttered:** 散らかった、雑然とした
> - **Modular:** モジュール式の、組み合わせ可能な
> - **Customizable:** カスタマイズ可能な
> - **Manufacturer's warranty:** メーカー保証
> - **Complimentary:** 無料の、サービスの
> - **Valued at:** 〜相当の価値がある
