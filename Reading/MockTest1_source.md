## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (E-mail)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]
**From:** Sarah Jenkins, GreenTech Solutions
**To:** All Staff
**Date:** April 20, 2026
**Subject:** Annual Office Picnic

Dear Team,

I am pleased to announce that our annual office picnic will be held on Saturday, May 15, at Oakwood Park. This event is a great opportunity for everyone to relax and get to know each other outside of the office environment.

The company will provide a variety of food and beverages, including vegetarian and vegan options. However, we ask that you **RSVP** by clicking the link in this email no later than May 5, so we can finalize the catering arrangements. Please also indicate if you will be bringing any family members.

In the event of rain, the picnic will be moved to the following Saturday, May 22. We hope to see you all there!

Best regards,

Sarah Jenkins
Human Resources Manager

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the main purpose of the e-mail?**
(A) To announce a change in a project deadline
(B) To provide information about a corporate event
(C) To introduce a new member of the HR team
(D) To explain a new policy regarding office snacks

**2. What are employees asked to do by May 5?**
(A) Suggest a location for the picnic
(B) Submit their monthly expense reports
(C) Confirm their attendance via a link
(D) Volunteer for the catering committee

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 本文の冒頭に "our annual office picnic will be held on Saturday, May 15" とあり、社内行事（ピクニック）の告知が目的であるため、(B) "To provide information about a corporate event" が正解です。
> 
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "RSVP by clicking the link in this email no later than May 5" とあります。「RSVP」は「返事をする」という意味で、リンクをクリックして出席を確認するよう求めているため、(C) "Confirm their attendance via a link" が正解です。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Annual:** 年に一度の、例年の
> - **Beverage:** 飲み物
> - **RSVP:** 返信をお願いします（フランス語の Répondez s'il vous plaît の略）
> - **Catering:** 出前、配膳、ケータリング
> - **In the event of:** 〜の場合には
