## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Memo)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**MEMORANDUM**
**To:** All Department Heads
**From:** Robert Chen, Operations Manager
**Date:** April 15, 2026
**Subject:** New Printer Policy

Effective May 1, all printing in the main office building will be subject to new guidelines. To reduce paper waste and lower operating costs, employees are required to print documents double-sided by default. Color printing will be restricted to client-facing materials only and must be approved by a department head.

Each department has been allocated a monthly printing budget. Usage will be monitored through the new PrintTrack system. Departments that exceed their monthly allowance will be required to submit a justification report to the Operations Department.

If you have any questions about the new policy, please contact operations@greentech.com.

---

### [Questions]

**1. What is the primary purpose of the new printing policy?**
(A) To introduce a new document management software
(B) To encourage departments to purchase their own printers
(C) To reduce costs and minimize paper waste
(D) To limit the number of employees who can use printers

**2. What must a department do if it exceeds its monthly printing allowance?**
(A) Purchase additional paper from the supply room
(B) Submit a justification report to the Operations Department
(C) Request special approval from the CEO
(D) Stop all printing for the remainder of the month

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** 第1段落に "To reduce paper waste and lower operating costs" と目的が明記されています。(A)のソフトウェア導入は手段の一つ（PrintTrack）であり目的ではありません。
>
> **2. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 第2段落に "will be required to submit a justification report to the Operations Department" とあります。"exceed their monthly allowance" ＝ 予算超過、"justification report" ＝ 正当化レポートを提出。
