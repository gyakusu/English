### Part 7: Double Passage (Advanced)
**Time Goal:** 5 mins

#### [Passage 1: Advertisement]
**State-of-the-Art Office Space Available at The Meridian Tower**

Are you looking to elevate your business profile? The Meridian Tower offers premium office suites in the heart of the financial district. 

**Features include:**
* High-speed fiber-optic internet
* 24/7 security and concierge service
* Floor-to-ceiling windows with panoramic city views
* Access to our exclusive rooftop lounge and fitness center

**Special Offer:** Sign a 24-month lease by June 1st and receive the first two months rent-free! Additionally, tenants signing this quarter will have the standard security deposit reduced by 50%.

Contact Julian Varga at `j.varga@meridiantower.com` for a private tour.

---

#### [Passage 2: E-mail]
**From:** Elena Rossi, CEO of Nexa Design Studios
**To:** Julian Varga
**Date:** May 12
**Subject:** Inquiry regarding office suites

Dear Mr. Varga,

I am writing regarding the advertisement for office space at The Meridian Tower. Our current lease at the North Plaza expires in late July, and we are looking for a more modern space that reflects our company’s growing reputation in the design industry.

We are very interested in the premium suites, specifically those on the upper floors. However, our budget is somewhat constrained by a major project expansion we are currently undertaking. The rent-free period mentioned in your ad is very appealing. Could you clarify if the reduced security deposit applies even if we opt for a shorter lease term, such as 12 months?

We would like to schedule a tour for next Tuesday afternoon. Please let me know if that works for you.

Sincerely,

Elena Rossi

---

#### [Questions]

**1. What is suggested about Nexa Design Studios?**
(A) It is currently facing financial difficulties due to a failed project.
(B) It plans to relocate its office before the end of July.
(C) It has more employees than its current office can accommodate.
(D) It is a startup company looking for its first office space.

**2. Which incentive is Ms. Rossi NOT certain she qualifies for?**
(A) The two months of free rent
(B) The high-speed fiber-optic internet access
(C) The reduction in the security deposit
(D) The access to the rooftop fitness center

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** Passage 2の冒頭に "Our current lease at the North Plaza expires in late July" とあり、現在の契約が7月末に切れるため、新しいオフィスを探していることがわかります。したがって、7月が終わる前に移転する計画であることが示唆されています。
> 
> **2. 正解: (C)**
> **解説:** Passage 1の広告では、敷金（security deposit）の50%減額は「今四半期中に契約するテナント」に適用されるとありますが、Passage 2でMs. Rossiは「12ヶ月という短い契約期間でも適用されるか」を質問しています。一方、2ヶ月無料（A）については「24ヶ月契約が条件」と広告に明記されており、彼女はその条件を知った上で「魅力的だ」と述べています。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Elevate:** 高める、向上させる
> - **Panoramic:** 全景の、パノラマの
> - **Concierge:** コンシェルジュ（受付・案内係）
> - **Lease:** 賃貸借契約
> - **Security deposit:** 敷金、保証金
> - **Expire:** 期限が切れる、満了する
> - **Constrained:** 制約された、不自由な
> - **Inquiry:** 問い合わせ

### 🧠 学習のポイント
> [!tip]- 難易度アップのポイント（解説）
> 今回の問題では、以下の要素を取り入れています。
> 1. **情報の照合（Cross-referencing）**: 1つ目の文書の条件（24ヶ月契約、今四半期契約）と、2つ目の文書の疑問点を照らし合わせる必要があります。
> 2. **言い換え（Paraphrasing）**: 選択肢(B)の "before the end of July" は、本文の "expires in late July" を言い換えたものです。
> 3. **NOT問題**: 確実なものと不確かなものを区別する、TOEIC高得点帯で頻出のパターンです。
