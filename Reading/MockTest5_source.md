## 📝 Part 7: Single Passage (Notice)
**Time Goal:** 3 mins

### [Passage]

**Notice to All Building Tenants**
**Re: Scheduled Elevator Maintenance**
**Hartwell Business Centre — Building Management**

Please be advised that the elevators in Block A will be undergoing scheduled maintenance on Thursday, April 24 and Friday, April 25, from 7:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M. both days. During this time, only elevator No. 3 will remain operational.

Tenants with heavy deliveries or moving activities planned for those dates are strongly encouraged to reschedule. The stairwells on both sides of the building will remain accessible at all times.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

For more information, contact the building management office at ext. 201.

---

### [Questions]

**1. When will the elevator maintenance take place?**
(A) Saturday and Sunday, April 26–27
(B) Thursday and Friday, April 24–25
(C) Every weekday for the next two weeks
(D) Monday through Wednesday

**2. What are tenants with scheduled deliveries advised to do?**
(A) Use the freight elevator on the ground floor
(B) Contact the building office at least one week in advance
(C) Complete all deliveries before 7:00 A.M.
(D) Consider rescheduling their deliveries

---

### 💡 解答・解説
> [!success]- 解答を表示
> **1. 正解: (B)**
> **解説:** 冒頭に "Thursday, April 24 and Friday, April 25" と明記されています。日付と曜日の両方が一致する(B)が正解です。
>
> **2. 正解: (D)**
> **解説:** "Tenants with heavy deliveries … are strongly encouraged to reschedule" とあります。"reschedule" ＝ 日程を変更する。"strongly encouraged" は命令ではなく強い推奨であることに注意。

### 🔑 重要語彙
> [!info]- 語彙リストを表示
> - **Undergo:** 受ける、経験する
> - **Operational:** 稼働中の、使用可能な
> - **Tenant:** テナント、借主
> - **Stairwell:** 階段の吹き抜け部分
> - **Accessible:** 利用可能な、アクセスできる
> - **Reschedule:** 日程を変更する
